Request for Information (RFI)

Advanced Deftech Private Limited welcomes Requests for Information (RFIs) from government organisations, defence forces, public sector undertakings, OEMs, and authorised industry partners.

RFIs may relate to product availability, technical capabilities, sourcing partnerships, compliance requirements, or potential collaborations.

All enquiries are handled with strict confidentiality and in accordance with applicable regulatory and contractual obligations.


When submitting an RFI, kindly include:

  • Organisation name and country
  • Authorised point of contact
  • Nature of requirement or enquiry
  • Indicative timelines (if applicable)
  • Any relevant technical or compliance references

Our team will review the request and respond through the appropriate official channel.
